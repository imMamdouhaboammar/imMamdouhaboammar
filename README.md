## What I work on

I care about systems that make teams faster, clearer, and less dependent on random prompting.

That usually means:

* Building AI-assisted workflows for agencies, marketers, and product teams
* Creating tools that help non-technical builders ship cleaner work
* Turning marketing strategy into repeatable operating systems
* Designing content, paid media, and CRO workflows that are easy to run
* Building prompt systems, skills, agents, and quality gates for AI coding

## How I think about AI work

Most AI output fails because the system around it is weak.

A better workflow needs:

1. Clear context
2. Strong constraints
3. Repeatable steps
4. Review before shipping
5. Memory of what worked and what failed

That is the direction behind most of my current open-source work.
